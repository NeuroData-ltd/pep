Automate your sanction and AML processes with our API solution

Companies are more and more under scrutiny to adhere to sanction regulations, identify Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and prevent Counter Terrorist Financing (CTF). Screenings are expected to happen at mutiple occassions during the customer life-cycle to not only Know Your Customer (KYC) once, but at any business relevant point in time. Starting from the customer onboarding, continuing for every financial transaction and then again at predefined cadences. The costs for screening and handling of false positives increase the cost base of a company while resulting in an ever thinner margin.
